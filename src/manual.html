Getting Started

This guide introduces the core concepts of FocusFlow, built around the Pomodoro Techniqueâ€”a proven time management method to boost productivity.

  1. Choose a task you want to focus on.
  2. Click the Start button to begin a 25-minute focus session.
  3. Work on the task without interruption until the timer rings.
  4. Take a short 5-minute break by clicking Short Break.
  5. After four focus sessions, take a longer 15-minute break using the Long Break button.
  6. Repeat the cycle and watch your productivity soar!

Timer Features

The main screen is your command center. Here's a detailed look at each function:

  • Start: Begins a standard work session. During this time, the website blocker is active.
  • Short/Long Break: Starts a break timer. The website blocker is automatically deactivated.
  • Pause: Pauses the current timer. This logs an "interrupted" session in your analytics.
  • Reset: Stops and resets the timer. This also logs an "interrupted" session.
  • Settings: Opens the window to customize timers and the website blocklist.

Analytics Dashboard

Click on "Today's Sessions" to open your dashboard and gain insights into your work habits.

  • Current Streak: Shows consecutive days you've completed at least one focus session.
  • Daily Focus Score: A percentage score of completed vs. interrupted sessions. Aim for 100%!
  • Achievements: Unlock badges for reaching milestones like "5-A-Day" or "Consistency King".
  • Productivity Heatmap: A calendar view of your focus intensity. Darker squares mean more completed sessions.

Settings & Customization

Tailor FocusFlow to your exact needs in the settings window:

  • Manage Blocklist: Add websites (one per line) to block during focus sessions.
  • Timer Durations: Customize the length (in minutes) for work, short breaks, and long breaks.

Click Save Settings to apply changes. The main timer updates instantly if it's not running.

Troubleshooting

Solutions for common issues to ensure a smooth experience.

Issue: The website blocker isn't working.

Solution: FocusFlow needs administrator privileges to block websites. Please close the app, right-click its icon, and select "Run as administrator".

Issue: My settings or analytics data seem to be gone.

Solution: Data is stored locally in your user directory. Ensure these files haven't been moved or deleted. Contact support if the issue persists.

Keyboard Shortcuts

Control the timer from anywhere on your desktop, even when FocusFlow is minimized.

  • Ctrl + Alt + S: Start the current timer.
  • Ctrl + Alt + P: Pause the current timer.
  • Ctrl + Alt + R: Reset the current timer.

Mini Mode

Mini Mode provides a compact, always-on-top view of your timer, allowing you to keep an eye on your session while working in other applications without distraction.

  • To Enter Mini Mode: Click the 'compress' icon button () on the main control bar. The window will shrink to show only the timer.
  • Always on Top: In Mini Mode, the window will always stay on top of your other applications.
  • Resizable: You can click and drag the edges of the Mini Mode window to resize it to your liking. The timer will automatically scale to fit.
  • To Exit Mini Mode: Simply click the 'expand' icon button () inside the Mini Mode window. The application will restore to its full, normal view.

Best Practices for Productivity

  • Minimize Distractions: Before starting, turn off phone notifications and close unnecessary tabs.
  • Plan Your Tasks: Decide what you'll work on before starting the timer.
  • Use Breaks Wisely: Step away from your screen. Stretch, get water, or rest your eyes.
  • Be Consistent: Use FocusFlow daily to build a strong habit and a long streak!
